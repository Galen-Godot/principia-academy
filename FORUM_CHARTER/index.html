Charter: Principia Forum

Purpose: The Intellectual Marketplace and Economic Engine.

Mission: To facilitate the exchange of high-fidelity assets, peer-review services, and specialized tools. Forum is the mechanism through which we direct economic forces toward decentralization and human interconnection.

Merchant Integrity Protocol: To ensure the ecosystem maintains its core values, all supported merchants and participants must adhere to the following:

  1. Integrity of Product: Products must provide genuine benefit to humanity and be grounded in truth.
  2. Value-Positive Practices: Business practices must be ethically sound and contribute positively to the economic substrate.
  3. Transparency: All transactions and claims are subject to the investigative rigor of Principia Veritas.

Focus: Asset tokenization, peer-to-peer scientific collaboration, and the curation of a marketplace that rewards wisdom and integrity.
