The Process
01 State the axioms. Identify the minimal ontological claims. Strip everything else.
02 Extrapolate without constraint. Follow every implication of the axioms, regardless of where they lead.
03 Find convergences. Where do independent lines of reasoning arrive at the same result? Those convergences are candidate truths.
04 Attack adversarially. The strongest version of every objection is applied. Steel-man, then destroy.
05 Augment and drill. What survives attack is refined toward equations. What doesn’t is discarded or flagged as open.

The Principia Substrata research program was developed using this process. The papers above are its current output. The pipeline is available for engagement on work serious enough to deserve the same treatment.
