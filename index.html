Principia

Human-in-the-loop AI research for deep technical work

Hypotheses  Non  Fingo

Principia is an agentic research system built to pressure-test difficult ideas, numerical workflows, and manuscript-grade claims.

We use theoretical physics as a benchmark domain because it forces rigor across reasoning, equations, code, citations, and writing. The goal is not to market speculative theory as the product. The goal is to build and demonstrate a serious AI-assisted research workflow under hard conditions.

For Partners See the Engine

What It Does

Structured adversarial review, literature discipline, numerical workflow hardening, and manuscript-oriented synthesis for difficult technical work.

Benchmark Domain

Theoretical physics is used as the benchmark because it exposes weaknesses quickly: loose claims, brittle numerics, citation drift, and reasoning failure.

Current Priority

Build a credible research-infrastructure surface for partners, pilot support, and technically serious collaboration.
