About Principia

A human-in-the-loop AI research system for deep technical work.

What Principia Is

Principia is an agentic research workflow designed to help humans pressure-test difficult technical work. It is built for disciplined reasoning, numerical audit, literature control, and manuscript-grade synthesis.

Theoretical physics is the current benchmark domain because it is unforgiving: equations, code, prose, and citations must remain mutually coherent. The purpose is not to market speculative theory as the primary product. The purpose is to build a serious AI-assisted research system under difficult conditions.

What It Does

Principia supports high-difficulty technical work through adversarial review, derivation checking, numerical workflow discipline, and structured drafting. It is intended as research infrastructure for work where error compounds and superficial fluency is not enough.

Why This Site Exists

This site is a public surface for the project: explaining the system, documenting outputs, and making partnership conversations possible. It is intentionally restrained. We prefer auditable work over inflated claims.
