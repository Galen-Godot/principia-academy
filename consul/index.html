The Practitioner

A decade of applied R&D experience developing and validating analytical, process, and experimental methods across regulated and research environments, including medical device and dialysis systems development at Fresenius Medical Care.

BS Microbiology and Chemistry, Weber State University. Author of four published papers in substrate mechanics and theoretical physics. Designer and operator of the Principia multi-agent AI research system — the engine underlying this platform.

Services

Method Development

Design and development of analytical, experimental, and process methods from first principles. Feasibility assessment, parameter optimization, and systematic documentation for research and regulated environments alike.

Method Validation

Full validation packages: specificity, linearity, accuracy, precision, robustness, and transfer. Technical writing and documentation for regulatory submissions, internal qualification, or publication-ready reporting.

AI Research Systems

Design and deployment of multi-agent LLM pipelines for scientific research, literature review, and adversarial analysis. Custom orchestration for research teams requiring systematic, reproducible AI-assisted inquiry.

Adversarial Paper Review

Theory stress-testing via the Principia gauntlet engine. Six independent analytical agents interrogate submitted work from distinct epistemic postures. Full falsification record delivered with the review.
